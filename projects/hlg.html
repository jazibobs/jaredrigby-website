Digital Signage - The HLG Car Team

DEVELOPMENT STATUS: ONGOING

HLG is a used car dealership in the Stockport area specialising in Mazda, Suzuki, Honda and Toyota.

I am currently tasked with designing and implementing a "silent salesman" digital advertisement board to display cycling information about the company 24/7.

I have proposed a Raspberry Pi based solution which will make effecient use of the space available by being highly portable and simple to set up.

Technologies used: html, css, javascript, jquery, raspberry pi, linux
